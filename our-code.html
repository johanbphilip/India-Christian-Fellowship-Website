Our Code

Our Code outlines our values and reflects our priority to God, to one another and to the world. Our Code informs the Purpose and the Mission that we strive to pursue and ensures that India Christian Fellowship remains a church that upholds the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to glorify God by pointing people to Jesus Christ, who alone is sufficient for our every need. Our greatest desire is to help people know Christ and fully receive His love. In response to His everlasting love, we as a church have a three-fold approach to our mission: Love God, Love One Another and Love Our World

Love God

Our first mission is to love God with all our heart, soul, mind, and lives. For this reason, in our gatherings, we have four priorities: First, we worship God by singing praises to Him with our hearts and our voices. Second, we teach the Word of God so that we might truly know Him and know how He wants us to love Him. Third, we seek God in prayer, we rely on His Spirit to enable us to fulfill His purpose for us. Fourth, we break bread together to remember His death and resurrection.

Love One Another

Our second mission is to love one another as members together in Godâ€™s family. For this reason, we have many opportunities for fellowship, friendship, and service in our church body to help us become a genuine family. We want each person who attends India Christian Fellowship to know that he or she is loved and needed.

Love Our World

Our third mission is to love God's Creation. We work to share the gospel in our local communities and cities. We are also committed to share the good news of Jesus Christ to all the Earth.
